Painting And Decorating

All interior / inside painting & decorating

Where specified and agreed, this includes the painting of ceilings, walls, woodwork (including doors and frames, windows and frames, dado and picture rails and skirting), any plaster mouldings like cornices or ceiling roses, fire surrounds, cupboards including louvre doors, built in units or wardrobes and floors. Doors, skirting and architraves as well as many other items of woodwork can be stained or varnished.

Paper Stripping and Paperhanging

We can hang lining paper to help hide rough walls and then decorate over this or indeed hand patterened paper over the top. We can hang the lining paper vertically or horizontally to suit either purpose.

Minor plaster repairs to allow for decoration

As well as access to a plasterer for larger jobs we can make minor repairs ourselves to prevent delays in getting the work done.

Floor painting, staining and varnishing

Floors can be painted for that ‘New England’ look as well as stained or varnished, we can also paint your staircase to complement a runner style carpet.

Tiling repair

We can make minor tiling repairs by removing old and broken tiles from within an existing floor, wall or splash back and replace with new tiles and then grout them in.

All exterior / outside painting and decorating

Where specified and agreed, this covers all masonry surfaces whether rough or smooth, woodwork including doors and windows, cladding etc can be painted or varnished. Soffits and fascia boards can be painted as well as downpipes and water goods like guttering for instance. Retaining and boundary walls are painted and clean lines given to make your property look smart and crisp. We can also pick out period detail to help revive your home.

Scaffolding organised

We have access to a reliable scaffolder who will always try and meet given dates for erecting and removing scaffolding – I am always happy to include the scaffolding cost within my quote where necessary. Depending on site access and height, full scaffolding may not be required – therefore works are completed by ladder and / or scaffold tower – in accordance with ‘The Work at Height Regulations 2005’. Touching in is always done at the end of the job where the scaffold poles meet the masonry surface.

Guttering cleaned out, repaired or replaced during exterior decoration

We can replace (either in full or partial repair) any defective pieces of guttering, clips or downpipe sections and we will clear out the guttering whilst we have access via the scaffolding and/or ladders.

Repointing of brickwork

As part of the exterior decoration we can cut out and replace old and disintegrated mortar from between the brick courses helping your home to keep the elements out.
